Our History

Founded in [23 May 1997], Noordelikes Rugby Club has grown into one of Pretoria's most respected rugby institutions. We began as a small group of rugby enthusiasts and have evolved into a full-fledged club with multiple teams competing at various levels.

Our Mission

To develop rugby talent, foster sportsmanship, and build strong community ties through dedication, hard work, and a shared love for the game.
